How to Join

Membership cost is $25 for the full academic year. Attend one of our training sessions or email any of the captains through the contact page to join. Becoming a member is a great way to get in shape, make new friends, and get involved in the community.

Benefits of Joining

  • Offical member of Triathlon Club
  • Able to attend structured practices
  • Meet new people who have a passion for working out
  • Become competant at swimming, biking, and running
  • Benefit the community through club events
  • Discounted Indoor Triathlon Price

Membership Requirements

  • UWEC student
  • Paid membership
  • Hard working
